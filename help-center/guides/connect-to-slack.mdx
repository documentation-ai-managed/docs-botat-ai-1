title: Connect botat ai to Slack
description: Enable botat ai responses in your Slack workspace.
---

## Overview

Let botat ai respond to messages in Slack.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Slack App">
    Go to Slack API, create an app, and install it to your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the Bot Token">
    Copy the OAuth Bot Token (starts with `xoxb-`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register with botat ai">
    ````bash
    curl -X POST https://api.botatai.com/v1/integrations/slack \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -d '{"token": "xoxb-..."}'
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
